Dynamic configurable dashboard

Tableau dashboard can be configured easily to meet your needs in the future.

Realtime notification and early warning

Arkana BI does not only provide you with a passive dashboard. With our notification system, all important data can be actively send to you via WhatsApp or email.

Daily target, reminder of outstanding tasks, or any suspicious data input can also be notified to you in real time.

Has separation of access rules

Arkana BI support configurable access rules for each dashboard.

You can restrict your dashboard access to related user, role or department in your company.

Target and Key Performance Indicator

Arkana BI provide you with a system to monitor and control your company KPI.

Define your KPI, target, and budget in your application, the BI will give you a daily report of your KPI progress in dashboard and send automatic notification via email or WhatsApp.
